Dailypills

DAILYPILLS is an online pharmacy for ongoing and chronic medications -- and makes them much simpler to manage. We sends a 14-day supply of medications, divided into daily packets -- at no extra cost.It even analyzes the combination of medications a person is taking to ensure they don't counteract each other.

Benefits

  • Save time (no need to queue or travel to the pharmacy)
  • We sort your pills and deliver them directly to your house.
  • Peace of Mind: Notification system to remind you when to take the next pill
  • One stop shop. Get everything you need in one place.
  • Cost: In difference from local pharmacies we don’t have retail costs which means we can guarantee the best prices in the market.
